ABOUT THIS MAP

Hello friend!


This map is a visualization of events happening at Smith College.


There are the five categories of events you can view:


  • Featured
  • Student Interest
  • Lectures and Symposia
  • Multicultural Events
  • Athletic Events


Pick on a category tab to get orange dots that appear where an event is taking place. The bigger the dot, the more events are taking place at that location.


Click on the dot, and you'll get a list of events happening there!


Feel free to look around and see what is happening on Smith's campus (updated daily).


And check out our github to find out how our map works!


Happy clicking!
